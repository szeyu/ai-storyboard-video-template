# Constraints

## Brand Constraints
- Must match kawaii / cute aesthetic of product packaging (orange box, cartoon lizard art)
- Tom Lizard character must stay consistent with the figure designs

## Legal / Compliance Constraints
- Do not claim specific pull rates for hidden edition
- No misleading unboxing (e.g., don't imply guaranteed hidden edition per box)

## Visual Constraints
- 9:16 vertical format
- Keep figures identifiable — don't distort or re-skin characters
- Reference images: `04-assets/source-images/alltype.webp`, `alltype2.webp`

## Audio Constraints
- Upbeat, cute BGM — no licensed music without clearance
- Minimal or no voiceover; let visuals + text carry the story

## Platform Constraints
- YouTube Shorts: max 60s, but targeting 15s
- Safe zone for text: avoid top/bottom 10% (UI overlap)
