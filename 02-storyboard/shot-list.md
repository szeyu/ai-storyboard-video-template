# Shot List — "Tom's Big Day"

## Arm Shake Guide
Tom's spring arms are the visual language of this video. Describe shake intensity in every shot note:
- **Gentle sway** — calm, curious, resting state
- **Active shake** — excited, reacting, engaged
- **Blur shake** — peak hype, overwhelming emotion
- **Sync shake** — multiple Toms shaking in unison (S06)
