## Must Stay Consistent Across All Scenes

| Trait | Description |
|---|---|
| Body | Bright lime green, chubby cartoon lizard / dinosaur body |
| Eyes | Wide white round cartoon eyes, large black pupils, slightly bulging |
| Mouth | Big open mouth, showing small white teeth, expressive |
| Arms | Spring-coil arms — small green hands at end of visible coil springs |
| Base | Black hexagonal platform base |
| Scale | Small desktop toy figure — roughly palm-sized |
| Art style | Kawaii vinyl toy figure, slightly glossy, 3D render or product photo quality |

## Per-Figure Unique Traits

| Figure | Unique Trait |
|---|---|
| Plain Tom | No accessory. Neutral open-mouth expression. Arms out. |
| Donut Tom | Holding two pink glazed donuts up to eyes — donuts replace eyes visually |
| Tongue Tom | Tongue sticking out long and dramatically downward |
| Beret Tom | Wearing a pink/rose beret on head. Tongue slightly out. |
| Hidden Edition Tom | Wearing pink oval sunglasses over eyes |
