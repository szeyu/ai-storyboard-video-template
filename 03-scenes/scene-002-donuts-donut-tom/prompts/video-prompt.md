# Video Prompt

## Purpose
S02 — 3 seconds. Donut Tom spots donut, arms shake wildly toward it.

## Prompt
Cute kawaii green cartoon lizard toy with giant pink donut eyes spots a large pink glazed donut. His spring-coil arms shoot forward shaking wildly and blurring with excitement — spring coils visibly stretching and vibrating. Donut eyes widen and spin. Body leans forward with desire. Warm pink bakery light. Pastel background. Energy escalates quickly.

## Motion Notes
- Arms rapidly extend and shake/blur toward donut
- Body leans slightly forward
- Donut eyes animate — spin or wobble

## Camera Notes
Close-up on face and arms. Slight tilt toward donut.

## Use First Frame?
Yes.

## Audio Direction
Music energy picks up. Light bouncy beat kicks in.

## Negative Prompt
Calm arms, dark scene, no donut visible.

## Notes
- Model: Seedance 2.0
- Duration: 3s
