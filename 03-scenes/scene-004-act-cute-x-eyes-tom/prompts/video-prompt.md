# Video Prompt

## Purpose
S04 — 3 seconds. Actcute Tom cuteness overload — arms wide, chaotic cute energy.

## Prompt
Cute kawaii green cartoon lizard toy with X-shaped cartoon eyes sways with arms stretched wide open — spring coils at full extension on both sides. The arms shake chaotically in wide swinging arcs. Confetti drifts down from above. He vibrates with cuteness overload. Pastel pink and lavender dreamscape background. Camera slowly pushes in.

## Motion Notes
- Arms swing wide in large arcs — not just vibration but full side-to-side sweep
- Body sways slightly with arm motion
- Confetti drifts down continuously

## Camera Notes
Medium shot. Gentle push in. Arms must be fully visible on both sides of frame.

## Use First Frame?
Yes.

## Audio Direction
Music becomes playful and dreamy. Confetti sparkle sound effect.

## Negative Prompt
Arms at sides, calm, dark background, X eyes not visible.

## Notes
- Model: Seedance 2.0
- Duration: 3s
