## Scene Name
Hook — Normal Tom

## Scene Purpose
Stop the scroll. Introduce Tom as a character. Calm before the chaos.

## Duration
2 seconds

## Visual Description
Normal Tom sits alone on a small wooden desk. Morning light. Soft bokeh background. He is completely still for half a beat — then his spring arms slowly begin to sway side to side. He looks straight at camera like he knows what is coming.

## Subject
Tom Lizard figure — normal pose. Bright green, round eyes, spring-coil arms, black hexagonal base.

## Background / Environment
Warm cozy desk surface. Soft bokeh room in background. Warm morning light from left side.

## Camera Direction
Medium close-up. Slow push in toward Tom. Starts slightly wider, ends tighter on his face and arms.

## Lighting
Warm morning light. Soft shadows. Golden hour feel.

## Style
Kawaii, cozy, cinematic product shot. 9:16 vertical.
