# Video Prompt

## Purpose
S01 — 2 second hook. Tom still, then spring arms begin gentle sway.

## Prompt
Cute kawaii green cartoon lizard toy figure on a small wooden desk. He stands completely still for one beat, looking at camera with knowing expression. Then his spring-coil arms slowly begin to sway side to side — gentle, rhythmic, like a bobblehead waking up. Camera slowly pushes in toward his face. Warm morning golden light. Soft bokeh background. Kawaii, cinematic.

## Motion Notes
- 0.0–0.5s: Tom completely still
- 0.5–2.0s: spring arms begin gentle left-right sway, slowly increasing amplitude
- Camera: continuous slow push in throughout

## Camera Notes
Medium close-up. Slow push in. Starts slightly wider, ends tighter on face.

## Use First Frame?
Yes.

## Audio Direction
Soft gentle music box or kawaii chime starts faintly. No voiceover.

## Negative Prompt
Sudden movement, fast cuts, multiple figures, dark scene.

## Notes
- Model: Seedance 2.0
- Duration: 2s
