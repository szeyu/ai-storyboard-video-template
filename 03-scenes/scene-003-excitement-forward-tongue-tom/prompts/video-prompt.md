# Video Prompt

## Purpose
S03 — 3 seconds. Tongue Tom hype machine — arms blur, energy peak.

## Prompt
Cute kawaii green cartoon lizard toy leans hard forward into camera, tongue hanging out dramatically. Spring-coil arms shake so violently they become a motion blur on both sides — invisible from speed. Stars and sparkles fly off the shaking arms. Pop-art burst background radiates energy. The frame barely contains him. Snap zoom in.

## Motion Notes
- Arms: full blur throughout — maximum shake speed
- Tongue: hangs out and wobbles slightly
- Background: burst rays pulse with energy

## Camera Notes
Snap zoom in at start. Holds tight on Tom for rest of scene.

## Use First Frame?
Yes.

## Audio Direction
Beat drops here. Peak music energy.

## Negative Prompt
Calm arms, soft scene, missing tongue.

## Notes
- Model: Seedance 2.0
- Duration: 3s
