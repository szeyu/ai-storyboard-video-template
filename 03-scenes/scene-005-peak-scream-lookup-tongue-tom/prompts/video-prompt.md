# Video Prompt

## Purpose
S05 — 2 seconds. Peak scream — maximum tremor, mouth to sky.

## Prompt
Cute kawaii green cartoon lizard toy in full meltdown — head tilted back, mouth wide open screaming toward sky, tongue raised up. Spring-coil arms shake at maximum intensity — full body tremor, the most violent shake of the video. Gold and rainbow light erupts from behind. Flash cut in at start. The entire frame vibrates with energy.

## Motion Notes
- Arms: most intense blur/tremor of entire video
- Head stays tilted back throughout
- Background: gold/rainbow light pulses and radiates

## Camera Notes
Extreme close-up. Tilt up slightly to follow screaming direction.

## Use First Frame?
Yes.

## Audio Direction
Music stinger / peak hit. Maximum audio impact moment.

## Negative Prompt
Calm arms, head forward, dark scene, soft background.

## Notes
- Model: Seedance 2.0
- Duration: 2s
