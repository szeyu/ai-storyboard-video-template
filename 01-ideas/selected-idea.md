# Selected Idea

## Selected Idea
I002 — "Tom's Big Day"

## Why This Idea Was Selected
Story arc keeps viewers watching to completion. Hidden Edition reveal lands with emotional payoff only because viewer followed Tom's journey first. Higher watch time = better Shorts algo push = more Shopee clicks.

## Target Audience
Blind box collectors. Kawaii figure buyers. Ages ~16–30.

## Core Message
Tom Lizard has 5 sides — each figure is a chapter of his story. The hidden edition is his final form. Which one will you pull?

## Visual Direction
5 scenes, each featuring one Tom figure in a cute animated/cinematic environment. Fast cuts with expressive text overlays. Escalating energy. Hidden Edition reveal = biggest visual moment (flash, sparkle, zoom).

## Risk Notes
- Must keep Tom character consistent across all 5 scenes — use product reference workflow before generation
- Hidden Edition reveal must feel earned, not random
